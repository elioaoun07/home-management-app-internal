# ERA — Future Vision Vol. 4

**Last updated:** 2026-05-02

The fourth volume. Strictly **practical**. Zero emotional tracking. ERA as a **walking secretary**, a **chief of staff**, an **all-knowing operator** who handles the friction of life so you don't have to.

> Vol. 1 → ERA's mind
> Vol. 2 → ERA's senses + soul
> Vol. 3 → ERA's body, home, world
> **Vol. 4 → ERA's hands. The doer. The fixer. The one who gets it done.**

Every feature here passes a single test: **"Would a brilliant human secretary do this for me?"** If yes, ERA does it.

**Tag legend:** **[S]** = Standalone module · **[J]** = Junction (bridges existing modules)
