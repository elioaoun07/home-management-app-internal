# ERA — Future Vision

**Last updated:** 2026-05-02

ERA is not a budget app with AI sprinkled in. ERA is **your second brain** — a proactive assistant that captures, remembers, anticipates, and acts on your behalf across every dimension of your life. These ideas push ERA toward what a personal AI assistant should actually feel like in 2026+.

---

## Guiding Principles

- **ERA remembers everything** — every preference, decision, conviction, aspiration becomes queryable
- **ERA speaks first** — the magic is in unprompted, perfectly-timed surfacing of the right thing
- **ERA captures ambiently** — speak, glance, tap; ERA files, structures, connects
- **ERA acts as your operator** — handle the boring stuff, prep the hard stuff, execute on rails

---

## 🧠 The Memory Engine

ERA's superpower is **continuity across time**. Not data — *narrative*.

| # | Feature | What it does |
|---|---------|--------------|
| M1 | **ERA Diary** | Every night, ERA auto-writes a 3-paragraph diary entry of your day from your transactions, completed tasks, chats, places visited. You can read your life back. |
| M2 | **Echo from Past Self** | "6 months ago you said you wanted to write more." Surfaces dormant aspirations at the right moment, not as nagging — as gentle continuity. |
| M3 | **Conviction Tracker** | When you state a value out loud ("I'll never buy another subscription"), ERA logs it. Catches the contradiction 3 months later. "You once said... still feel that way?" |
| M4 | **Decision Archive** | Every meaningful decision (skipped vacation, declined invite, bought the laptop) auto-logs *the reasoning at the time*. Six months later: "Why did I do that?" → ERA tells you. |
| M5 | **Ask ERA About Me** | Natural language query over your entire life: "When did I last talk about pottery?" "What did I spend on coffee in 2024?" "Show me everything I've said about my career." |
| M6 | **Identity Statements** | A library of self-defining statements you've made over time. "I'm the kind of person who..." ERA references them when relevant decisions arise. |

## 🎙️ Ambient Capture

You shouldn't open an app to *use* ERA. You should *talk* to it like a thought partner.

| # | Feature | What it does |
|---|---------|--------------|
| C1 | **Brain Dump Mode** | One tap (or a hot phrase). Talk freely for 5 minutes — driving, walking, in the shower. ERA extracts every task, expense, idea, person mentioned, decision and files them in the right modules. |
| C2 | **Morning Pages** | Wake up, open ERA, dictate stream-of-consciousness. ERA structures into: today's intent, lingering tasks, emotions, ideas. Becomes searchable journal. |
| C3 | **Quick Capture Inbox** | A single place: "I just had a thought." ERA classifies later (task / idea / expense / question / wish). You don't sort — ERA does. |
| C4 | **Conversation Continuity** | Start talking on watch, continue on phone, finish on desktop. ERA remembers the open thread. No "what was I saying?" |
| C5 | **Voice Note Library** | Every voice note searchable by content. "Find that voice note where I talked about the trip idea." ERA transcribes + indexes everything. |

## 🔮 Anticipatory Intelligence

ERA's job is to surface the right thing **before you think to ask**.

| # | Feature | What it does |
|---|---------|--------------|
| A1 | **Pre-Meeting Briefing** | 15 min before a meeting/call: who you're seeing, what you last discussed, any pending threads, suggested talking points based on your shared history. Drops as a notification. |
| A2 | **5-Minute Fit** | You have 5 free minutes? Tap and ERA picks one perfectly-sized task: "Call dentist now — they close in 20." Context-aware: location, energy, time-of-day. |
| A3 | **Pattern Interrupt** | ERA learns your regretted patterns. About to order takeout for the 4th Wednesday in a row? Soft prompt: "Pantry has ingredients for pasta in 12 min. Override?" |
| A4 | **Predictive Inventory** | "You'll run out of coffee Tuesday at 10am based on consumption rate. Adding to Sunday's grocery list." ERA tracks consumption, doesn't wait for you to notice. |
| A5 | **Catch Me Up** | Coming back from vacation, sick day, deep work? Tap "Catch me up" → 90-second briefing: what changed in your world, what's urgent, what you missed. |
| A6 | **Re-emergence Alerts** | "You haven't called your mom in 3 weeks. Last time you discussed her doctor's appointment — want to follow up?" Relationship maintenance, proactively. |

| # | Feature | What it does |

## ⚡ Decisional Operator

Hand entire decision categories to ERA. It pre-thinks, you approve.

| # | Feature | What it does |
|---|---------|--------------|
| D1 | **Pre-Decided Inbox** | ERA sees a recurring bill went up 8%. Flags it. Drafts the cancel/negotiate email. You approve in one tap. ERA handles the routine; you handle the exceptions. |
| D2 | **Decision Rehearsal** | Big decision coming? "Walk me through it." ERA: "Imagine you said yes. How do you feel? Now imagine no. What did your past self say about this kind of choice?" |
| D3 | **Three Options, Ranked** | "Should I X?" → ERA returns three responses tailored to your context, ordered by best fit, with the trade-offs. You pick. No more 30 min decision paralysis. |
| D4 | **Conviction Test** | Before any purchase >$X, ERA: "Last time you bought at this price unplanned, you returned it. Want a 24h hold?" Self-knowledge as a circuit breaker. |
| D5 | **Negotiation Scripts** | "Time to renegotiate internet." ERA: phone number, current plan, competitor offers, what to say, predicted objections, fallback positions. Fully prepped. |

## What This Vision Does Differently

Other apps **track**. ERA **knows you**.
Other apps **alert**. ERA **anticipates**.
Other apps **store**. ERA **remembers narratively**.
Other apps **wait for input**. ERA **surfaces unprompted**.

The North Star: ERA should feel like **a thoughtful friend who has read every diary entry, attended every meeting, sat through every grocery trip — and remembers all of it perfectly**.

Budget, items, recipes, hub chat, NFC — these are just *the substrate*. ERA is the layer that turns them into one continuous, intelligent narrative of your life.

## Building Order (Suggested)

1. **M5 (Ask ERA About Me)** — unlocks the value of all your existing data, no new collection needed
2. **C1 (Brain Dump Mode)** — biggest input-friction reduction, makes ERA feel alive
3. **A1 (Pre-Meeting Briefing)** — first "wow, it knew" moment for daily use
4. **M1 (ERA Diary)** — emotional hook, retention multiplier, demoable
5. **X1 (Hypothesis Engine)** — the moment ERA stops being a tool and becomes a presence

Everything else compounds on these foundations.
