# ERA — Future Vision Vol. 3

**Last updated:** 2026-05-02

The third volume of ERA's evolution. None of these features overlap with Vol. 1 or Vol. 2.

> Vol. 1 gave ERA a **mind**.
> Vol. 2 gave ERA **senses + soul**.
> Vol. 3 gives ERA the **body, the home, and the world** it inhabits with you.

Less abstract. More tactile. The dimensions of a real human life that no productivity app touches: how you sleep, how you eat, how you dress, the car you drive, the block you live on, the year you're spiritually in. Each layer is a fresh "wow, the app does *that* too?" moment.

**Tag legend:** **[S]** = Standalone module · **[J]** = Junction (bridges existing modules)

## 🧬 1. Biorhythm Layer

ERA learns *when* you are at your best — biological rhythms, not just calendar time.

| # | Feature | Type | What it does |
|---|---------|------|--------------|
| BR1 | **Energy Peak Map** | [S] | A weekly heatmap of your actual energy by hour. Built from how/when you complete tasks, log expenses, send messages. ERA literally maps your circadian shape. |
| BR2 | **Decision-Quality Hours** | [J] | ERA notices that decisions made between 3–6pm tend to get reversed within 48h. Suggests: "Sleep on this until tomorrow morning." |
| BR3 | **Cycle-Aware Planning** | [S] | For users who track menstrual cycles: ERA aligns task intensity to phase. Heavy cognitive work in follicular, lighter logistics in luteal. Discreet, opt-in, never patronizing. |
| BR4 | **The Witching Hour Detector** | [S] | Identifies your daily low ebb (the "I shouldn't be making decisions" window) and silently lowers ERA's nudge volume during it. |
| BR5 | **Cognitive Prime Window** | [J] | Auto-protects 90-min focus block at your sharpest hour by default. Tasks marked "hard" auto-route there. |
| BR6 | **Seasonal Self** | [S] | Tracks how *you* shift across seasons: winter productivity vs. summer creativity vs. fall introspection. Plans your year around your actual seasonal pattern, not a generic productivity calendar. |
| BR7 | **Recovery Index** | [J] | Combines sleep, task load, and physical activity into a 0–100 score. ERA defers heavy asks when score is low. |
