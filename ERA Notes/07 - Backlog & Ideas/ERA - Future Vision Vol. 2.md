**Last updated:** 2026-05-02

Continuation of `ERA - Future Vision.md`. Pushing into new dimensions: ERA's eyes, ERA's social brain, ERA's voice, ERA's mirror, and the layers that make ERA feel less like software and more like a presence.

## 👁️ ERA's Eyes (Visual & Spatial Capture)

ERA doesn't just hear you — it sees what you see.

| # | Feature | What it does |
|---|---------|--------------|
| V1 | **ERA Lens** | Point camera at anything: a fridge, a receipt, a bookshelf, a wine label, a confusing form, a parking sign. ERA reads it, files it, acts on it. The universal input. |
| V2 | **Screenshot Brain** | Every screenshot you take is parsed: appointment? auto-add. flight? auto-track. Wi-Fi password? auto-save. tweet you'll forget? auto-thread. You stop having a 2,000-screenshot photo library. |
| V3 | **Photo Memory** | Every photo gets indexed by content. "Show me the parking spot photo from the airport last March." "What did that menu look like at the Italian place?" Your camera roll becomes a searchable archive. |
| V4 | **The Mirror Read** | Take a photo of your fridge / closet / desk. ERA tells you what's expiring, what you haven't worn in 6 months, what's cluttering. Reality-check via image. |
| V5 | **Document Vault** | Snap any official document (lease, ID, insurance card, prescription, warranty). ERA OCRs, indexes, extracts dates, sets renewal reminders. End of "where did I put that paper." |

## 🤝 ERA's Social Brain

You have a relationship CRM in your head — except it's leaky and inconsistent. ERA fixes that.

| # | Feature | What it does |
|---|---------|--------------|
| R1 | **People Memory** | Every person in your life gets a profile ERA quietly maintains: last contact, recurring topics, what they care about, important dates, things they've recommended to you. Never blank-slate a conversation again. |
| R2 | **Social Capital Ledger** | Subtle bidirectional tracking of who's done what for whom. "You haven't seen Sami in 4 months — last time *he* paid for dinner, *he* drove. Your turn to initiate." Healthy reciprocity, not score-keeping. |
| R3 | **Birthday Choreography** | 3 weeks before any tracked person's birthday: ERA orchestrates gift idea (based on past conversations), reservation suggestion, message draft, day-of reminder. You become "the friend who remembers." |
| R4 | **Inner Circle Drift** | "You used to talk to Maya weekly. It's been 7 weeks. Drift or drift-by-design?" Forces honesty about who's actually in your circle. |
| R5 | **The "Who Should I See" Engine** | Free Friday night? ERA suggests the 3 people most overdue + most-likely-available + most-likely-to-energize you. No more defaulting to the same person every time. |
| R6 | **Conversation Carryover** | "Last time you saw Dad you discussed his knee surgery. Mom mentioned cousin's wedding. Karim asked about your project." Pre-loaded context for any human encounter. |

## 🗣️ ERA's Voice (Communication Operator)

ERA writes in **your** voice, not a corporate template.

| # | Feature | What it does |
|---|---------|--------------|
| W1 | **Draft Mode** | Any message that needs a thoughtful reply: ERA drafts it in your voice (learned from your past messages). You edit a word, hit send. Sounds like you, not like AI. |
| W2 | **Tone Translator** | One message, three tones: warm / direct / playful. Pick the one that matches the recipient. Stop agonizing over wording. |
| W3 | **Difficult Conversation Prep** | Need to fire someone, decline a wedding, ask for a raise, say something hard? ERA rehearses with you: opening line, anticipated reactions, three branch responses, fallback. Walk in calm. |
| W4 | **Saying No Library** | A growing collection of *your* graceful declines, ranked by relationship depth. Reuse the patterns that work for you. |
| W5 | **Apology Engine** | Wronged someone? ERA helps you craft an apology that's specific, accountable, and not defensive — based on actual research, not just sweet talk. |
| W6 | **The Voice Note Reply** | Someone sent you a long voice note. ERA transcribes it AND drafts a voice-note-style reply for you to record over. Closes the loop in 30s. |

| # | Feature | What it does |

## 🌀 ERA's Recovery Protocol (Failure & Setbacks)

Other apps quit you when you slip. ERA leans in.

| # | Feature | What it does |
|---|---------|--------------|
| RC1 | **Compassion Mode** | ERA detects you're struggling (skipped tasks, low engagement, terse responses) → softens automatically. Pauses non-essential nudges. Surfaces only the gentlest wins. |
| RC2 | **The Reset Sequence** | After a bad day/week: tap "reset." ERA proposes a 3-step recovery protocol customized to you (a walk + a small task + a clean-slate tomorrow plan). |
| RC3 | **Survival Receipts** | "You've felt this way 4 times before. You bounced back in 5 days, 8 days, 3 days, 6 days. You'll bounce back this time too." Past-self as proof. |
| RC4 | **The Failure Library** | Catalog of things that didn't work — habits abandoned, projects shelved, goals missed. Not for shame: for pattern recognition. "I tend to abandon things at week 3 — what if I plan for week 3 differently?" |
| RC5 | **Vent Channel** | A private channel where you can rant with zero consequence. ERA listens, lets you exhaust the feeling, doesn't action anything unless you say so. |
