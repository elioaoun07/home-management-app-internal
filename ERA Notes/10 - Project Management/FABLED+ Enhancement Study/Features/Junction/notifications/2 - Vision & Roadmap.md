# Notifications · Vision & Roadmap

> [FABLED+ root](<../../../_index.md>) · **Notifications** · [1 · Feature State](<1 - Feature State.md>) · [2 · Vision & Roadmap](<2 - Vision & Roadmap.md>) · [3 · Action Plan](<3 - Action Plan.md>) · [4 · Checklist](<4 - Checklist.md>)

## Enhancement thesis

Make notification intelligence causal and testable: group one root cause into one story, shadow-evaluate new rules, and let users inspect the interruption contract.

## Business and household value

Precision protects the right to interrupt. Fewer, more complete stories raise action rate and prevent notification fatigue as the app becomes proactive.

Measure attention returned, risk reduced, and outcomes improved—not engagement.

## Roadmap

1. Now — define causal keys and shadow metrics for one noisy class.
2. Next — deliver one grouped alert story with evidence and policy explanation.
3. Later — require shadow evaluation and an interruption contract for every new proactive rule.

## New opportunity set

### V1 — Causal alert story

- **Mechanism:** Group symptoms sharing root entity/time/cause into one evolving narrative and next decision.
- **Smallest proof:** Group a due item, calendar mismatch, and schedule pressure case.
- **Success measure:** Alert count drops while required actions are preserved.
- **Kill criterion:** Use simple grouping keys if causal inference is unreliable.
- **Invariant:** Urgent independent causes are never hidden.

### V2 — Shadow delivery lab

- **Mechanism:** Run a rule without notifying, record would-deliver timing/content/policy outcome, and compare to user action.
- **Smallest proof:** Shadow one new rule for 14 days.
- **Success measure:** Precision and volume meet a predeclared threshold before launch.
- **Kill criterion:** Do not launch if value cannot be proven.
- **Invariant:** Shadow mode has no push/send capability.

### V3 — Interruption contract

- **Mechanism:** Expose reason, evidence, urgency rule, quiet-hours behavior, suppression, and owner per class.
- **Smallest proof:** Create contracts for critical and informational classes.
- **Success measure:** Users can predict when each class interrupts and tune it once.
- **Kill criterion:** Use a concise Why this alert sheet if full settings are excessive.
- **Invariant:** Changing contract is audited and person-scoped.

## Existing-roadmap boundary

Delivery policy, digest, feedback learning loop, action inbox, household-aware delivery, and notification→conversation are existing plans; this pack adds causality and pre-delivery validation.

## Strategy guardrail

Start read-only or shadowed; use existing proposal and mutation owners; earn automation.
