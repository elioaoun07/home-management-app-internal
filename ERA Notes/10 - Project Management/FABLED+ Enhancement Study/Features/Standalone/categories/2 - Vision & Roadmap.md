# Categories · Vision & Roadmap

> [FABLED+ root](<../../../_index.md>) · **Categories** · [1 · Feature State](<1 - Feature State.md>) · [2 · Vision & Roadmap](<2 - Vision & Roadmap.md>) · [3 · Action Plan](<3 - Action Plan.md>) · [4 · Checklist](<4 - Checklist.md>)

## Enhancement thesis

Treat categories as a living decision language: small enough to capture quickly, stable enough for history, and expressive enough to guide action.

## Product and business value

A clean taxonomy lowers capture friction and improves every budget, analytic, and AI answer. The value is not more categories; it is fewer corrections and more stable household meaning.

Value should be measured in avoided corrections, prevented surprises, shorter decisions, safer shared action, or attention returned—not page visits.

## Roadmap

1. Now — calculate a read-only taxonomy health report from existing categories and transactions.
2. Next — separate durable intent tags from display hierarchy without recategorizing history.
3. Later — propose merges or seasonal overlays only as reversible previews.

## New opportunity set

### V1 — Taxonomy health score

- **Mechanism:** Measure unused nodes, correction frequency, overlap, depth, and concentration without judging spending.
- **Smallest proof:** Generate the score for both users and inspect the five worst nodes.
- **Success measure:** At least one simplification reduces capture hesitation or corrections.
- **Kill criterion:** Keep only raw diagnostics if a composite score obscures more than it explains.
- **Invariant:** No automatic rename, merge, or historical recategorization.

### V2 — Intent tags over display labels

- **Mechanism:** Allow stable meanings such as essential, joy, household, or personal to sit beneath mutable category names.
- **Smallest proof:** Tag ten categories locally and test analytics questions.
- **Success measure:** One cross-category decision becomes simpler without adding capture steps.
- **Kill criterion:** Drop tags if they duplicate budget groups or require constant maintenance.
- **Invariant:** Tags are additive metadata; existing category IDs remain canonical.

### V3 — Seasonal taxonomy overlay

- **Mechanism:** Temporarily promote relevant choices for Ramadan, travel, holidays, or school periods while preserving historical labels.
- **Smallest proof:** Use a local overlay for one event and compare category-selection time.
- **Success measure:** Median selection time improves with no increase in miscategorizations.
- **Kill criterion:** Remove if users rely on search/recent choices instead.
- **Invariant:** Overlay order never changes stored category identity.

## Relationship to existing plans

Merchant intelligence is covered elsewhere; this vision focuses on the quality and adaptability of the classification language itself. These proposals complement the baseline rather than renaming its ideas.

## Strategic boundary

Do not add a second engine, bypass the proposal/draft pattern, weaken household visibility, or automate a state change before its shadow proof and inverse action are written.
