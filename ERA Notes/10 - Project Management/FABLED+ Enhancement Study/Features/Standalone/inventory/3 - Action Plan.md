# Inventory · Action Plan

> [FABLED+ root](<../../../_index.md>) · **Inventory** · [1 · Feature State](<1 - Feature State.md>) · [2 · Vision & Roadmap](<2 - Vision & Roadmap.md>) · [3 · Action Plan](<3 - Action Plan.md>) · [4 · Checklist](<4 - Checklist.md>)

## Objective

Prove one closed-loop improvement without widening trusted mutation boundaries.

## Now

- Define confidence and freshness as a pure selector.
- Choose ten items with different volatility.
- Measure current correction and restock history quality.

**Gate N:** deterministic fixture or read-only proof; no behavior change.

## Next

- Render verified/estimated/unknown and optional zone.
- Trial a short verification sweep.
- Record correction reasons without adding capture fields to every mutation.

**Gate X:** vertical slice with provenance, partner scope, offline behavior, retry identity, and inverse.

## Later

- Calibrate decay only from real confirmations.
- Expose low-confidence status to shopping/recipes as uncertainty.
- Never auto-consume stock without a reviewable rule.

**Gate L:** measured outcome earns scale; otherwise keep the proof or kill it.

## Mandatory preflight

- Read Feature Map, vault docs, consumers, and mutating routes.
- Run Design Doctrine Ten Questions.
- Name cache, time, household, idempotency, and Undo behavior.

## Non-goals

Self-driving shopping, pantry-aware recipes, unit canonicalization, barcode/OCR, and waste tracking are prior ideas; this pack focuses on inventory truth.
