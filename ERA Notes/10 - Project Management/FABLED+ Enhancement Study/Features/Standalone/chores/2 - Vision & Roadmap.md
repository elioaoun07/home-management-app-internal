# Chores · Vision & Roadmap

> [FABLED+ root](<../../../_index.md>) · **Chores** · [1 · Feature State](<1 - Feature State.md>) · [2 · Vision & Roadmap](<2 - Vision & Roadmap.md>) · [3 · Action Plan](<3 - Action Plan.md>) · [4 · Checklist](<4 - Checklist.md>)

## Enhancement thesis

Evolve chores into a fair, low-friction household workload system that protects capacity without turning home life into performance management.

## Business and household value

Conflict avoided and invisible labor made legible are the value. Success is fewer reminders and resentments, not maximum completed rows.

Measure attention returned, conflict avoided, and outcomes improved—not engagement.

## Roadmap

1. Now — estimate effort and handoff semantics in a read-only weekly review.
2. Next — trial claim/handoff and minimum viable chore sets on stressful days.
3. Later — propose fairness adjustments from explicit feedback, never automatic scoring of people.

## New opportunity set

### V1 — Effort-weighted fairness lens

- **Mechanism:** Compare time, frequency, unpleasantness, and coordination as ranges rather than a single competitive score.
- **Smallest proof:** Review two weeks together using local annotations.
- **Success measure:** Both people agree the summary is fair enough to guide one rebalance.
- **Kill criterion:** Drop numeric aggregation if it feels judgmental; keep workload discussion prompts.
- **Invariant:** Never rank a person's worth or publish a winner.

### V2 — Claim and handoff protocol

- **Mechanism:** Represent offered, claimed, handed off, declined, and acknowledged states with reasons and expiry.
- **Smallest proof:** Use it on five shared chores.
- **Success measure:** Fewer off-app messages are needed to know who owns work.
- **Kill criterion:** Collapse to assigned/unassigned if state overhead exceeds benefit.
- **Invariant:** Silence never assigns responsibility.

### V3 — Minimum viable home

- **Mechanism:** Define a tiny protected slate for overloaded days and intentionally defer the rest.
- **Smallest proof:** Create one bad-day preset and use it once.
- **Success measure:** Critical chores happen without a cascade of overdue noise.
- **Kill criterion:** Keep manual bulk postpone if the preset is never used.
- **Invariant:** Health/safety tasks cannot be silently demoted.

## Existing-roadmap boundary

Generic overdue triage, intent-aware planning, and gamification are deliberately not repeated; this pack centers fairness, consent, and capacity.

## Strategy guardrail

Start read-only or in shadow mode. Persist, notify, or automate only after a real proof passes its gate.
