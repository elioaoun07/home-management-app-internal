## Objective

Prove one closed-loop improvement without widening trusted mutation boundaries.

## Now

- Confirm the missing logger choke point and current structured writers.
- Classify console/silent-catch sites by user impact.
- Choose one frequent recoverable workflow.

**Gate N:** deterministic fixture or read-only proof; no behavior change.

## Next

- Implement fingerprint and receipt as a small vertical slice.
- Add one sanitized failure replay fixture.
- Route new logs through one explicit interface.

**Gate X:** vertical slice with provenance, partner scope, offline behavior, retry identity, and inverse.

## Later

- Ratchet console counts down rather than attempting a risky bulk rewrite.
- Track time-to-resolution and preserved work.
- Promote repeated causes into fix-bug known-cause docs.

**Gate L:** measured outcome earns scale; otherwise keep the proof or kill it.

## Mandatory preflight

- Read Feature Map, vault docs, consumers, and routes.
- Run Design Doctrine Ten Questions.
- Name cache, time, household, idempotency, privacy, and Undo behavior.

## Non-goals

Generic error-log persistence already exists; this pack emphasizes causal grouping, recovery, and conversion into protection.
