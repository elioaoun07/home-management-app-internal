# Preferences · Action Plan

> [FABLED+ root](<../../../_index.md>) · **Preferences** · [1 · Feature State](<1 - Feature State.md>) · [2 · Vision & Roadmap](<2 - Vision & Roadmap.md>) · [3 · Action Plan](<3 - Action Plan.md>) · [4 · Checklist](<4 - Checklist.md>)

## Objective

Prove one closed-loop improvement without widening trusted mutation boundaries.

## Now

- Build a preference inventory with scope, default, consumer, cache, and impact.
- Choose one high-impact setting for preview.
- Define precedence for temporary versus later explicit choices.

**Gate N:** deterministic fixture or read-only proof; no behavior change.

## Next

- Render provenance and impact on the chosen setting.
- Test both users and multiple devices.
- Add exact rollback receipt.

**Gate X:** vertical slice with provenance, partner scope, offline behavior, retry identity, and inverse.

## Later

- Trial one expiring override.
- Measure reversals and unexpected effects.
- Propose defaults only from repeated explicit choices.

**Gate L:** measured outcome earns scale; otherwise keep the proof or kill it.

## Mandatory preflight

- Read Feature Map, vault docs, consumers, and routes.
- Run Design Doctrine Ten Questions.
- Name cache, time, household, idempotency, privacy, and Undo behavior.

## Non-goals

Theme implementation, Google sync setup, and general onboarding already exist; this pack adds policy scope and explainability.
