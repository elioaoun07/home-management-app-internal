## Objective

Prove one closed-loop improvement without widening trusted mutation boundaries.

## Now

- Inventory watch actions, confidence, and network dependencies.
- Specify haptic/visual states for local, synced, review, and failure.
- Measure current interaction time.

**Gate N:** deterministic fixture or read-only proof; no behavior change.

## Next

- Prototype one resumable draft handoff.
- Add offline receipt and retry visibility.
- Remove or demote one interaction that exceeds the glance budget.

**Gate X:** vertical slice with provenance, partner scope, offline behavior, retry identity, and inverse.

## Later

- Measure watch-origin capture completion and corrections.
- Keep only high-value actions.
- Test partner/privacy behavior on visible complications or notifications.

**Gate L:** measured outcome earns scale; otherwise keep the proof or kill it.

- Read Feature Map, vault docs, consumers, and mutating routes.
- Run Design Doctrine Ten Questions.
- Name cache, time, household, idempotency, and Undo behavior.
