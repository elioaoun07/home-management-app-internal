# Recipes · Action Plan

> [FABLED+ root](<../../../_index.md>) · **Recipes** · [1 · Feature State](<1 - Feature State.md>) · [2 · Vision & Roadmap](<2 - Vision & Roadmap.md>) · [3 · Action Plan](<3 - Action Plan.md>) · [4 · Checklist](<4 - Checklist.md>)

## Objective

Prove one closed-loop improvement without widening trusted mutation boundaries.

## Now

- Add source/confidence labels to imported recipe fixtures.
- Choose one timer-heavy recipe for a critical-path model.
- Define cooking outcome fields: timing, taste, substitution, keep/change.

**Gate N:** deterministic fixture or read-only proof; no behavior change.

## Next

- Render the truth envelope in edit/review.
- Prototype critical-path cooking as an optional mode.
- Mark one tested variant approved without deleting history.

**Gate X:** one vertical slice with provenance, partner scope, offline behavior, retry identity, and inverse.

## Later

- Measure repeat-cook success and mid-cook edits.
- Use outcomes to propose version changes as drafts.
- Never generalize food-safety advice beyond verified rules.

**Gate L:** measured outcome earns persistence and scale; otherwise keep the smaller proof or kill it.

## Mandatory preflight

- Read Feature Map, vault docs, and mutating routes.
- Run Design Doctrine Ten Questions.
- Name cache, time, household, idempotency, and Undo behavior.
- Invoke recurrence or other risk playbooks where applicable.

## Non-goals

Pantry-aware cooking, unit canonicalization, leftovers, AI reports, and generic ontology are prior ideas; this pack focuses on recipe truth and execution outcome.
