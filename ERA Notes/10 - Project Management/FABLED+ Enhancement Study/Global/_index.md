# Global · FABLED+ Index

> [FABLED+ root](<../_index.md>) · **Global** · [1 · Feature State](<1 - Feature State.md>) · [2 · Vision & Roadmap](<2 - Vision & Roadmap.md>) · [3 · Action Plan](<3 - Action Plan.md>) · [4 · Checklist](<4 - Checklist.md>)

> **Verdict:** The app is no longer feature-poor. It is a 40-feature household operating system with unusually strong capture, money invariants, two-person identity, and physical-world interfaces. Its next 10× is to close the loop after capture: expose truth quality, rehearse machine decisions, govern shared choices, verify outcomes, and learn without silently taking authority.

## Global loop-readiness score

| Dimension | Score | Why |
|---|---:|---|
| **Truth** | 3/5 | Strong invariants and documentation culture; several authoritative constants and mapped paths have drifted. |
| **Capture** | 4/5 | Form, Hub, voice, NFC, watch, imports, guest, recipes, shopping, and offline paths are exceptional. |
| **Decision** | 3/5 | Analytics, ERA, drafts, and planners help; evidence/confidence is not a universal contract. |

| **Total** | **17/30** | Strong operating system, incomplete closed loop. |
